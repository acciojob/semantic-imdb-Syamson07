Tom Cobb is a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's subconscious through dreams. He is given a chance to erase his past crimes if he can accomplish an impossible task: implanting an idea into someone’s mind—Inception. As the mission unfolds, Cobb struggles with his own mind and the haunting memory of his wife.
